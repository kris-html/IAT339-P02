Interactive Elements

In-text link

CSS selector:

.a

Sample code:

<a href="https://www.youtube.com/">YouTube</a>

Rendered element:

YouTube

Navigation Link

CSS selector:

.button-nav

Sample code:

<a href="#" class="button">Default Button</a>

Rendered element:

Default Button

Submit/Reset Buttons

CSS selector:

.button1, .button2

Sample code:

<button type="submit" value="Submit">Submit</button>
<button type="reset" value="Reset">Reset</button>

Rendered element:

Text Input Field with Label

CSS selector:

.input [type=text]

Sample code:

<label for="entertext">Enter Text:</label><br> <input for="text" id="entertext" name="entertext">

Rendered element:

Radio Button with Label

CSS selector:

.checklist

Sample code:

<label class="checklist">
<input type="radio" id="red" name="bestcolor" value="Red">
</label>

Company Branding Ideas

What does my company do?

My company supplies digital illustration products, tools, and accessories online.

Who is my company to potential customers?

My company appeals for people needing new tools or products because theirs is worn down or lost, or for people getting into this new hobby and wanting the right tools for the job.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

My company offers a wide variety of products in one location in a quick and efficient way without needing to search all over. Getting products to their front door is fast and quick.
